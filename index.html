Projects

May 2016

Search and Recovery Robot - SRR Prototype

Designed, built and programmed a robot, using LEGO EV3 Mindstorms software, that autonomously navigated through a lunar surface simulation completing various tasks such as pressing buttons, crossing and climbing through rough terrain and hills, retrieving power supplies, traversing through debris formed tunnels, and avoiding obstacles. MS Project was employed to plan, track and update the project's progress and Microsoft Excel served to calculate a cost estimate. The logo, initial sketches, and final designs of the robot were created using Auto CAD and LEGO Digital Designer.

June 2016

Tech-Maker

Conducted hands-on STEM related activities with over 60 young people from 3rd to 12th grade at the DreamYard Maker Jam at Lehman College. Specifically designed a mini project/competition for students to create bridges and dams using newspaper and other materials. The bridge or dam which could hold the most textbook wins. This project was designed to teach students about the fundamentals of structural engineering and to inspire them to pursue careers in Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics (STEM).
